White Space and Clean Design

Apple

apple.com

Apple does a great job using the white space between every element to give breathing room for their audience. There are no extra content that isn't needed. The design is simple and straight to the point.

Visual Hierarchy

slack.com

The slack homepage uses a great way of showing visual hierarchy. They show the importance of using a larger font to show their audience what the most important section in the website.
